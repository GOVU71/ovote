About US

Online Voting System is to provide an alternative voting channel to increase voter participation, reduce election cost while upholding the highest security, verifiability and integrity standards.

Major benefit of this system is that it increases voting percentage dramatically and election results can be declared instantaneously.

In traditional elections, voter has to go to polling station in person to cast his/her vote, where as through our application, voter can cast vote directly from the place they live without being attending the polling station

When we vote, we take back our power to choose, to speak up and to stand with those who support us and each other.
